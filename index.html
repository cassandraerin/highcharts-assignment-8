Crime Rates In The U.S.

The fluctuation of crime in the United States is important to keep track of because it is often a good indication of what laws are working or need to be improved on in order to reduce crime rates. 2015 was a good indication that some kind of change is needed due to the spike in murder and nonnegligent manslaughter in the U.S. The FBI catagorizes violent crime as ofenses that involve force or threat of force. These include murder, rape, robery and aggravated assault.

FBI Department Of Justice crime in the U.S.

Violent crime includes the deaths of police officers. Police deaths are catagorized by the types of duties the officers were performing at the time of their death, which often overlaps with the violent crimes in the U.S.
